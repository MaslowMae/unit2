.:.About:.:.

Brittany is originally from Arizona where she has moved to Colorado for 5 years and now is off to Florida! She has worked in financial compliance for over 10 years and has never stopped learning. She has a Master of Science degree in Clinical and Forensic Psychology and is certified in Anti-Money Laundering and Program Management. She has worked for financial institutions MSBs, investment firms and finTech where she has worked with diverse product sectors each having their own vulnerabilities and risk appetites.

One of her greates acccomplishements was building a risk score model where she working with top level engineers to turn it into a ML model and AI. She has worked with SQL to work with big data and reporting and hopes to keep building her data and coding skills to be a true asset to the field of finTech and the a leader in the growing use of AI.
